Motivation

We are driven by a deep understanding of the importance of cooking and the necessity of sustenance. We recognize the challenges that arise when certain ingredients are unavailable or unsuitable due to allergies or regional differences, especially in Thai cuisine. Traditional search engines often fall short in providing specific information about these unique and specialized ingredients. That's why we develop a web application that utilizes an ingredient ontology. This tool captures the relationships and properties of Thai ingredients, providing suitable substitutions without compromising on flavor, texture, or nutritional value. Our goal is to make cooking more accessible and enjoyable, while also reducing food waste. This is our motivation to make cooking more accessible, efficient, and enjoyable for everyone.

Web Description

Our IngOn, proposes an innovative approach through the development of an ingredient ontology which is a structured knowledge base tailored to the nuances of Thai ingredients, utilizing Description Logics for precision. Our web application is designed to empower cooks of all levels, offering a user-friendly way that allows people who use the platform to navigate in our website in order to make their recipe customization easily based on dietary preferences and restrictions. By focusing on Thai local ingredients and their substitutions, our project aims to not only preserve the authenticity of dishes but also contribute to cost-effective and health-conscious culinary solutions.
